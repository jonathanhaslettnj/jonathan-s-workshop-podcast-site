How to Prevent Tools from Slipping Off a Dowell
A simple trick that keeps tools stable and prevents accidental drops. Start making the wooden rings using a half inch bit, to match the half inch diamter of the dowel. Then use a 3/4" hole saw to cut a ring arond the 1/2" hole. Then cut 3/8" inch thickness on the table saw, and the rings will fall out.
